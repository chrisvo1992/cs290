Instructions

Welcome to the Guess who,what, or where game. Your job will be to guess what person, object, or country is being described in the clues. You will first choose which category you want to play. Three categories:
  • Guess WHO we are describing
  • Guess WHAT we are describing
  • Guess WHERE we are describing

You will be given main clues about your pick. If you still don't know your pick you can click the hint button and an extra hints will be provided. You will only be given 2 extra hints about your pick.

You will type your answer in the textbox and click the submit button to enter your guess. If you guessed incorrectly you will see an appear at the bottom.
You have 5 chances to guess your pick correctly. If you have 5 "X"s you will lose the game!

You will have a chance to submit what difficulty level you though your pick was to give us feedback.


If you are ready for a challenge please navigate the toolbar above and choose which category you want to play!
